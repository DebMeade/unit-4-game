Crystals Collector!

Do you think you'll be able to hit the target number?

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

By clicking on one of the crystals below you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by making your total score equal to the target number and you lose the game if your total score goes above the target number.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the values associated to the crystals will change.
